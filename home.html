About Us

We are RoboTech, Leander High School's BEST Robotics team. We are a team working together and using our unique skills to build and design a robot for this year's theme, Current Events.

Our Processes

First, we divided our team into 2 groups: design/contruction, and an auxillary group, which did everything else like t-shirts, web design, and logo design. This enabled us to get more done, much faster. Next, we created a basic design of Namora, and then continued correcting its flaws and working on it since. One of the challenges we had this year was building the arm grabbing mechanism. After several tests, we decided on the final design.

BEST Robotics Theme

This year's theme is "Current Events". The task given was to build a device that could pick up plastics and other garbage in the sea, whilst avoiding obstacles and keeping balance.

About Our Robot

Our robot this year is called Namora. The goal of our robot is to collect trash from the sea, and Namora is known as the Sea Queen in the Marvel Universe, so we thought the name fit. TALK ABOUT DESIGN
